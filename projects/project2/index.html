Hello there! Welcome to this little collection of sewing and craft tips through which you may, hopefully, find inspiration and useful information that would encourage trying out some interesting projects or help you keep your outfits for longer, perhaps adding some extra pizzaz to them as desired.

Wouldn't it be cool to have the ability to fix up an outfit all on your own, picking the fabric, fit and feel? What about patching up that pesky hole in your favorite pair of jeans? The posts on this site will guide you through the necessary steps and point you towards great resources on your way to saying, "oh it's just a popped button, a worn collar or a hole in my shirt; I can figure something out ". Take the above dress for example: explore the various YouCanDIYit pages and see if you can find resources that may help you take on this project or even improve on the outfit's design!
